🏡 House Rules – LA LEGIUN STRANIERA

Your holiday home, just a few small rules…

⏰ Check-in and Check-out Times

Check-in: from 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM
📩 Please inform us in advance of your expected arrival time.

Check-out: from 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM

❌ Cancellations and Payments

Cancellation and prepayment policies may vary depending on the type of accommodation.
📅 We recommend checking the specific conditions when booking.

👶 Children and Beds

Children of all ages are welcome.
To see correct prices and occupancy info, please specify the number and age of children in your booking.

🚫 No cribs or extra beds available.

🆓 Minimum Age

There is no age requirement for check-in.
Everyone is welcome!

💵 Payments

Payment can be made in cash upon arrival.
💡 We recommend bringing cash for any extras.

🚭 Smoking

Smoking is not allowed inside the property.

🎉 Parties and Events

Parties and events are not allowed.

🐾 Pets

Pets are not allowed. 🐾

ℹ️ Additional Information

The property is not available for bachelor or bachelorette parties or similar events.

📅 Seasonal Availability

Note: The property is open from March to October.
During winter, only stays longer than 15 days are considered.

Thank you!
